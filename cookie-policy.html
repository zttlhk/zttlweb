Understanding How We Use Cookies

Last Updated: March 2026

Contact: contact@zenith-terra.com

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Zenith Terra Tech Limited uses cookies for the following purposes:

2.1 Necessary Cookies (Essential)

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of these cookies.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
cookie_consent Stores your cookie consent preferences 1 year
session_id Maintains your browsing session Session

2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences and choices.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
language_pref Remembers your language preference 1 year
form_data Temporarily stores form data Session

2.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics - throttles request rate 1 minute

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services:

3.1 hCaptcha Cookies

We use hCaptcha for form security. hCaptcha may set cookies to:

  • Verify human users and prevent spam
  • Improve security detection algorithms
  • Maintain challenge-response state

hCaptcha cookies are classified as functional/necessary for security purposes. For more information, please refer to hCaptcha's Privacy Policy.

3.2 Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect anonymized usage data.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

4.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:

  • Accept All: Allow all cookie types
  • Only Necessary: Only allow essential cookies

4.2 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

4.3 How to Clear Cookies

To clear cookies from your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

5. Cookie Duration

The cookies we use have different lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (1 minute to 2 years) or until you delete them
